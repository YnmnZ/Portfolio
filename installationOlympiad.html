This is a prototype for an installation art. The installation art is based on a modified version of a worldview I created. (For the original version of the worldview, I am working on a personal wiki. Go to Working On section for more information.) The worldview and the installation art are inspired by cyberpunk and dystopian medias and mythologies.

The installation consists of two parts: physical set-up and digital video. Since this is a prototype, the set-up is not complete. I created advertisements in the worldview with reference to actual advertisements.

The team wrote scripts and made videos for three different scenerios of a person living in this world. We consider this as an immersive experience that consists of both physical and digital works.
