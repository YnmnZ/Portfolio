This is a photo collection I made during my first year in NYU. The collection consists of both conventional and experimental photos.

The photos are taken around the Brooklyn Campus of NYU. All photos are processed to be black&white.

The work consists of multiple photos. The photos shown in this page are selected from the complete collection. For the complete photo collection, please refer to the link on the left.
